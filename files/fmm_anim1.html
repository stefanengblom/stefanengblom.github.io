Cylinder flow



Impulsively started flow around a circle at Reynolds number Re = 140 with development of the instability and transition to Von Karman vortices. The large picture below shows the overall Von Karman street at the end of the computation.

References

  • The fast multipole method was further detailed in S. Engblom: On well-separated sets and fast multipole methods, in Appl. Numer. Math. 61(10):1096--1102, 2011: (doi).
